Although I more commonly go by just "Rielle," my name is Rielle Marie Quejada. I am the youngest of four siblings and the only one in my family who doesn't need glasses in their day to day life. While I am a mathematics major with an intent to go into education, my interest in programming is growing after having taken CIS 103 and 180 last year. That combined with my love for design and art overall led me to enroll in CIS162: HTML and CSS! I'm looking forward to the projects in this class.

One of my favorite websites is Fighter's Block, which is a simple writing app that gamifies writing. You set your word count goal, which becomes the enemy's health, and your own fighter's health ticks down while you're idle. I use it to get ideas in order and find it helps me avoid overthinking.
